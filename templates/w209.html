Does Different Dating App Behavior Influence Success?

An exploration of behavioral patterns, engagement, and match outcomes 💗🌿

Overview

We analyzed how user behavior on dating apps relates to outcomes like matches, engagement, and messaging success.

The dataset captured interaction patterns including swipe behavior, messaging activity, profile engagement, and match outcomes such as ghosting and catfishing.

Our goal was to understand whether common behaviors (like app usage time, demographic data, or profile content) actually lead to better dating outcomes.
