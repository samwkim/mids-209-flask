Does Different Dating App Behavior Influence Success?

An exploration of behavioral patterns, engagement, and match outcomes 💗🌿

Overview

We analyzed how user behavior on dating apps relates to outcomes like matches, engagement, and messaging success using a dataset of synthetic users.

The dataset captures swipe behavior, messaging patterns, profile quality, and interaction outcomes such as ghosting and catfishing.

The goal is to test whether common “advantages” like usage time, demographics, or profile completeness actually lead to better dating success.
